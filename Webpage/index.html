Connecting Gardens To People With Kípos

Kípos is an all encompassing smart gardening system that includes data acquisition, interpretation and visualisation that allows the user to take better care of their gardens. We use smart prediction analytics to provide the most optimal time to nourish your plants, while reducing your ecological footprint.
